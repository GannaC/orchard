At a glance

The busiest park in this set is Yosemite.

Scale

Distinctive fact

Patterns across the six featured parks

Largest footprint

Olympic leads this set at 922,651 acres.

Highest visitation

Yosemite drew the most visitors in 2024 with 4.12 million.

Best airport access

Redwood has the tightest air-to-park gateway here, with Del Norte County Airport about 4 miles from Crescent City Information Center.

Best wildlife personality

Olympic and Redwood are the strongest bets for Roosevelt elk, while Joshua Tree shifts the experience toward desert specialists.
